Some activities we do in the High School Salvador Moncada

Science

Students perform experiments, using the microscope to discover the microscopic living creatures.

Patriotism and Arts

We celebrate the Independence Day by marching with the Honduras flag, accompany by the martial band.

Pickling Industry

Students are taught how to preserve vegetables, by pickling them; also, they make jam, with tropical fruits like pineapples, mangoes, and guava.
